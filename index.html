About Merlin Rise

Merlin Rise Sports City

Merlin Rise Sports City is a luxurious residential complex located in Rajarhat by Merlin Group. It’s an Under Construction project, spread over an area of 15 acres.  It has 10 towers with a range of 2BHK (632Sqft, 639Sqft) and 3BHK ( 812Sqft) apartments.

Choose a life that is best and different not better. In our day-to-day life, there is no scope to live life freely. Time is running away and a step into a different lifestyle makes you active and joyful. Looking for a luxurious residential complex? Well, Merlin Rise is the right choice for you that goes beyond your imagination. It is an under-construction project. The project will be delivered by Jun 2027.

Merlin Rise Sports City is equipped with various resident-centric amenities that include Reserved Parking, Gymnasium, and Power Backup. For families with kids, there is a Children’s Play Area and kid’s Pool, nearby apart from Swimming Pool and sports Area. Tennis courts, Cricket Pitches, Squash courts, Volleyball courts, Skating Rinks, Aerobics Rooms, Basketball Courts, Badminton courts, Cycling & Jogging Track – are some avenues for sports lovers. Residents can make use of the Barbecue Area, Library, Restaurants/ Cafeterias, Internet / Wi-Fi, and provisions in the project. There is 24×7 Security. It is a Gated Community. There is provision for Open Car Parking, Closed Car Parking, and Visitor Parking.
